About

Hello, my name is Benjamin Stigsen, and welcome to my site.

This site is being used as a personal playground. As a place to create, store and document the development of my current life as well as my future life. This includes things like documenting my programming journey, setting goals for myself, planning out parts of my future and journaling of thoughts and dreams. I draw a lot of inspiration from Hundred Rabbits in their philosophy in regards to minimalism and efficiency, and hope to one day integrate it into my life with the same elegance as they have.

As of writing this I'm currently a 20 year-old student from Denmark, studying for the Higher Technical Examination Programme.
